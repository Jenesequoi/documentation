=======
Website
=======

Odoo provides a user-friendly website builder that empowers you to create and manage your online
presence effectively. Enhance lead generation and conversions effortlessly while ensuring seamless
maintenance. Odoo covers all your needs from web design to comprehensive reporting and
:abbr:`SEO (Search Engine Optimization)`.

.. cards::

   .. card:: Webdesign
      :target: website/web_design
      :large:

      Odoo's editing tools help tailor your website to fit your business needs. Select from a wide
      selection of pre-designed templates, building blocks, themes, color schemes, fonts, and more.


   .. card:: Pages
      :target: website/pages
      :large:

      Customize your website's structure by fine-tuning menus and adding pages. Boost your SEO
      performance for improved ranking.

   .. card:: Configuration
      :target: website/configuration
      :large:

      Access a vast collection of high-quality, royalty-free images from our 3-million-strong
      Unsplash database. Additionally, set up Google Search Console, your cookies bar, manage
      translations, and handle multi-websites functionalities.

   .. card:: Reporting
      :target: website/reporting

      Efficiently manage website analytics and integrate link trackers for comprehensive insights.

   .. card:: Mail groups
      :target: website/mail_groups

      Easily configure mail groups for effective communication.

.. important::
   Odoo offers a :ref:`free custom domain name <domain-name/register>` to all Odoo Online databases
   for one year. Visitors can then access your website with an address such as `www.example.com`
   rather than the default `example.odoo.com`.
